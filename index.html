About me

Who is
Angelos?

UX design is the intersection of logic, empathy, and creativity. Design is about making the complex simple, revealing the invisible, and creating digital experiences that feel human.

Every decision I make is grounded in an understanding of people, their needs, behaviors, and how they make choices. My background as a teacher has given me deep insight into how people learn, interpret, and interact, something I carry into every project.

UX for me

UX is fundamentally about understanding human behavior. Years in the classroom taught me how people process information, form habits, and make decisions under pressure. I bring that lens into every project, grounding design choices in behavioral psychology and cognitive principles rather than assumptions.

My ambition

To continue deepening my expertise in UX research and UI design, eventually growing into a Lead Product Designer role, combining design skills with leadership to create experiences that genuinely make a difference.

My approach

I believe in collaboration, flexibility, and openness to feedback as the keys to developing solutions that work for both the team and the users.
